STRUCTURE

Boys Team Event:
1. 5 Match Format. Singles-Singles-Doubles-Singles-Doubles.
2. Best of 5 amongst the above matches.
3. Team should consist of minimum 5 and maximum 6 players.
4. Only Two Players may repeat across the tie sheet.
5. The repeating players will only play ONE Singles and One Doubles.
6. Tournament will be conducted on Yonex AS2 Shuttles.

Girls Team Event:
1. 3 Match Format. Singles Doubles Singles.
2. Best of 3 amongst these matches.
3. Team should consist of minimum 2 & maximum 4 players.
4. Repeating player will play only ONE Singles and One Doubles.

RULES

Main Rules



All UG and PG students are allowed (Age should not be more than 24 And no complete

4. There will be separate events for girls and boys based on categories like singles, doubles, and mix.
5. Sports shoes are compulsory.
6. Tournament will be conducted on YONEX AS2 Shuttles.
